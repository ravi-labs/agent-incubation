Agent 03 · Participant Engagement

Life Event Anticipation
Agent

"Converts a reactive service model into a proactive one — at a scale no call center team can achieve."

The problem today
Before

We react when participants call

Right now, life event guidance only happens when a participant initiates contact — usually months after the event, when options may be more limited. A participant who terminates employment might call about their rollover six months later. A new parent might never update beneficiaries at all. The service model is entirely reactive.

After

We reach them at exactly the right moment

The agent monitors signals continuously. When it detects a life event — or anticipates one is imminent — it initiates the right conversation within hours. The participant gets relevant, actionable guidance at the moment it matters most, without ever having to call in.

Life events the agent detects and acts on
💼

Job termination

Signal: employer data feed termination record

Rollover conversation initiated within hours of termination. Participant receives options, timelines, and a guided decision flow before assets go dormant.

👶

New dependent / family change

Signal: beneficiary update or employer HR feed

Prompts beneficiary review, contribution adjustment, and life insurance coverage check — all contextualized to the participant's current financial picture.

🎂

Age milestones

Signal: participant age data

At 50, 55, 59½, and 62 — catch-up contribution eligibility, Medicare, Social Security strategy, and RMD planning conversations are surfaced proactively.

📊

Large allocation or contribution shift

Signal: unusual plan activity pattern

Sudden behavioral changes often signal a life event in progress. The agent investigates context before crafting a relevant outreach — not a generic alert.

How it works — three specialized agents in sequence
A1
📡

Signal Monitor Agent

Continuously ingests plan activity, employer data feeds, age milestones, and beneficiary changes. Clusters signals into probable life event patterns and scores confidence.

Always on
A2
✍️

Outreach Generator Agent

When a life event is detected, generates personalized outreach content — tailored to the specific event, the participant's account context, and their communication history. Drafts the message and selects the channel.

Triggered
A3
🔁

Follow-Through Tracker Agent

Monitors whether the participant took action after outreach. If not, escalates with a follow-up sequence. Feeds outcome data back to improve event detection and message effectiveness.

Feedback loop
Example scenario — job termination response timeline
T+0 hrs

Signal detected

Employer data feed indicates participant termination. Signal Monitor Agent scores confidence at 97%.

T+2 hrs

Outreach generated and delivered

"Hi Jordan — it looks like you may have recently left your employer. Your retirement account balance of $84,200 has a few options you'll want to consider in the next 60 days. Here's a quick guide to help you decide what's right for you."

T+72 hrs

No action — follow-up triggered

Participant hasn't opened the message. Follow-Through Tracker escalates with a second touchpoint and a simplified one-action CTA.

T+5 days

Action taken — loop complete

Participant initiates rollover. Outcome recorded. Signal model updated with confirmed event pattern.
