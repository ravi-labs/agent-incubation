    Fiduciary Watchdog Agent
    "Real-time fiduciary oversight at scale — currently impossible without this."
    Runs continuously against investment lineups, fee standards, participation rates, and DOL/ERISA thresholds. When something drifts, it drafts a plain-language finding and recommended remediation — the memo a compliance officer would spend hours writing.

    Life Event Anticipation Agent
    "Converts a reactive service model into a proactive one — at a scale no call center team can achieve."
    Monitors plan activity, employer feeds, age milestones, and beneficiary changes to anticipate life events before they happen. A participant who terminates employment gets a rollover conversation initiated within hours — not when they call six months later.
