Retirement Trajectory
Intervention Agent

"Personalized retirement interventions for millions of participants simultaneously — at a level of care no human team could ever match."

The problem today
Before

Tools show — but don't act

Most retirement platforms show participants their balance and a projection chart. That's where it ends. If someone is behind, there's no intervention unless they call in or a counselor happens to review their account. At scale, personalized outreach is simply impossible for any human team.

After

The agent monitors, identifies, and intervenes

The agent continuously watches behavioral signals across the entire participant base. When it identifies someone at risk of a poor retirement outcome, it generates and delivers a personalized, plain-language intervention — automatically, at any scale, before it's too late to change the outcome.

What the agent does
📡

Continuous behavioral signal monitoring

Tracks missed contributions, allocation drift, login frequency, age-to-balance ratio, and peer cohort comparisons across millions of participants in real time.

🎯

At-risk identification

Scores each participant against projected income replacement targets. Surfaces those whose trajectory has materially worsened before year-end or decade-end intervention windows close.

✍️

Personalized intervention generation

Drafts plain-language, participant-specific messages. Not generic nudges — specific projections with concrete actions: "Three changes could get you from 54% to 78% income replacement."

🔄

Outcome tracking feedback loop

Monitors whether each intervention led to action (contribution change, reallocation, login, call). Refines targeting and messaging based on what actually moves behavior.

Example intervention — what a participant receives
Generated intervention message

"Hi Marcus — based on your current contribution rate and account balance, you're on track to replace about 54% of your pre-retirement income. That's below the 70–80% most financial planners recommend. Three changes could close most of that gap: increasing your contribution by 2%, shifting 15% of your allocation to a diversified growth fund, and enabling auto-escalation so your rate grows with your salary. Together, these could get you to 78%. Want to make these changes now?"

Generated autonomously. Delivered within 24hrs of trajectory signal. No advisor involved.
